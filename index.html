About Genshin Impact


Genshin Impact is a new open world adventure RPG developed by miHoYo. You will explore a fantasy world called "Continent of Teyvat" in the game. In this vast world, you can travel through seven kingdoms, meet companions with different unique personalities and abilities, fight together against powerful enemies, and embark on a path to find find a loved one, or you can wander aimlessly and immerse yourself in a world full of life. Let your curiosity drive you to discover all the secrets in this world... Until you reunite with your loved ones and witness the deposition of everything in the final destination.
